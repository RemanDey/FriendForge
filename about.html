About FriendForge and Me

Welcome to FriendForge! This application was created out of a desire to enhance personal connections by providing a simple, private way to remember and manage the unique aspects of our friends.

In our increasingly digital world, it's easy to lose track of the subtle nuances that make each friendship special. FriendForge aims to bridge that gap, allowing you to quickly recall details like a friend's sense of humor, their favorite foods, or even their punctuality. This isn't about categorizing people, but rather about building stronger, more thoughtful relationships by being more attentive and remembering the things that matter to them.

My Motivation

I built FriendForge as a personal project to explore and solidify my skills in **front-end web development**. It's a demonstration of practical application using:

  • **HTML5:** For structuring the content.
  • **CSS3:** For modern, responsive, and dynamic styling (including the dark mode theme and animations).
  • **JavaScript:** For all the interactive functionalities, data management (using `localStorage`), and dynamic content rendering.
  • **Font Awesome:** For intuitive and appealing icons.

This project helped me understand data persistence in the browser, dynamic DOM manipulation, and building a clean, user-friendly interface. It represents my passion for creating useful tools that blend functionality with appealing design.
